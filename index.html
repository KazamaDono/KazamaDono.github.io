About

Nour, a.k.a Ayukotsu, is a security researcher who has exploited dozens of vulnerabilities and bugs throughout his journey. His mission is to become a competent 0 Day Engineer exploiting vulnerabilities within the Windows Operating System, and within AI related systems. He is the Founder of Spectra VRG, Lead Developer in Anarchy, Mentiforce resident, Ex-OSINT Analyst on Tracelabs, and CTF Player. Nour has collaborated with ThePentrix to build automated offensive tools, created an ecosystem for infosec and safe information disclosure, and aided in operations where open-sourced intelligence on criminals where needed. Transitioning into AI/ML engineering, Nour intends to create scalable solutions that interweve Exploit Development, research, and AGI. With the hopes of better understanding human behaviour and psychological profiling, his taken an initiative to study Neurocognitive Intelligence under NCI University.
